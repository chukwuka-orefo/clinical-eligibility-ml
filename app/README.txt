Clinical Eligibility Tool

This tool supports screening and prioritisation of patients for
clinical trial eligibility in secondary-care settings.

How to run:

1. Ensure a WinPython distribution is available in the folder:
   WinPython\

2. Double-click:
   run_app.bat

3. Your web browser will open automatically.

4. Click "Run eligibility screening" to generate results.

Outputs:

- Results are written to the "outputs" folder.
- Files are provided in CSV format for clinical review.

Important:

- This tool does NOT make clinical decisions.
- Outputs must be reviewed by qualified staff.
